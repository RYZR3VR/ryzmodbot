Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 December 2025

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how ryz’s Mod bot (“the Bot”) handles user data when interacting on Discord.

2. Data Collection
The Bot may log:
- Message content
- User IDs
- Server information

This data is collected to support moderation features, respond to commands, and improve functionality.

3. Data Retention
Logged data is retained for 30 days unless required longer for moderation or troubleshooting. After this period, data is deleted or overwritten.

4. Data Usage
Collected data is used only for:
- Responding to commands
- Enforcing server rules
- Improving bot functionality

5. Data Sharing
The Bot does not sell or share logged data with third parties. Data may be disclosed only if required by law or to comply with Discord’s Terms of Service.

6. Security
Data is stored securely and access is limited to the developer. The Bot relies on Discord’s infrastructure for message delivery and storage.

7. User Control
Users can stop using the Bot at any time by removing it from their server or group chat. Logged data will be deleted according to the retention policy.

8. Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes implies acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact
